Our Story

We are headquartered at Bangalore, India. As each and every organization have their own story of ups and downs, same is ours and one fine day a group of friends having different skills set, diverse background and passionate enough to do all that makes their client happy decided to start up with something which is today known as MY VA 247 registered as MY VA SERVICES PRIVATE LIMITED. Since then we are growing continuously.

Our Mission

We are passionate to deliver quality services to our clients. The core motto is to work on building trust,loyalty and an ever going relationship with our clients by fulfilling their each and every requirement of day to day life as well as professional ones, letting them focus on things which require their physical presence. Always ready to take up a challenge or do something new and yes we do customize ourselves depending upon our clients need. The team believes in the smart work rather than going the conventional way of hard work. We clearly say a big NO for anything which is not legal to do. We are ready to learn, modify and explore with your instruction and guidelines. Anything which is new, adventurous, and can be handled remotely is more than welcome and will definitely love to work on such projects and assignments. We believe in the saying “Action speaks louder than words”.
